About Me

As a current graduate student pursuing MS in Business Analytics and Information Management from Purdue University, I am passionate about merging Analytics and Business knowledge to generate actionable insights that add value to the companies. In a world of ever-growing data and ever-changing trends, I strongly believe Analytics will foster a decorum of business comprehension by depoliticizing business decisions. I come from a Mechanical engineering background. Upon graduation, I worked as a Business analyst in the healthcare sector, where I learned key skills like gathering Business requirements, defining precise business problem, using analytical thinking and statistical analysis to arrive at solutions feasible to the business problem on hand. Consequently, I worked as an Analytics manager for a management consulting company and was responsible for Project delivery and Client management wherein I picked crucial skills like Thought leadership, People’s management, Written communication and Project management. Lastly, my passion to identify business problems, find patterns, and carefully deduce business insights aspires me to be the Sherlock Holmes of the analytics world. Hence, I aim to be part of an organization where I can further strengthen my analytical skills, build on my business acumen and make a difference to the company.
